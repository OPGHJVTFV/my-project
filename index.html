1. Introduction

This Agreement outlines the terms and conditions under which the Creator will provide promotional content for the Brand, as managed by the Agency. The Agency acts as a liaison between the Brand and the Creator to ensure the successful execution of the promotional campaign.

2. Scope of Work & Deliverables

The Creator agrees to produce, deliver, and publish social media content endorsing the Brand's products/services:

  • Total Deliverables: Seven (7) Reels (May Campaign)
  • Posting Frequency: Two (2) Reels per month until total deliverables are met.

3. Payment Terms

In consideration of the full and satisfactory performance of the deliverables outlined above, the Creator shall be compensated as follows:

  • Total Compensation: ₹20,000
  • Advance Payment: 50% (₹10,000) will be paid in advance upon the approval of the demo content.
  • Final Payment: 50% (₹10,000) will be paid upon the successful completion and publishing of all required deliverables.

4. Content Guidelines & Usage

  • The Creator must secure written approval for the demo from the Brand/Agency prior to any public posting.
  • Content Retention: The Creator must keep the content live on their profile and ensure it is not deleted, archived, or hidden for at least five (5) days.
  • Usage Rights: The Creator grants the Brand and the Agency the full usage rights to repurpose and utilize the generated content for promotional purposes.
